Our Menu

Chicken

Our chicken dishes are marinated for 12 hours in a blend of garlic, ginger, paprika, yogurt, and lemon juice. We use only free-range chicken, grilled or oven-baked to maintain juiciness and depth of flavor.

Popular Dishes:
- Grilled Tandoori Chicken
- Creamy Butter Chicken
- Crispy Fried Chicken

Main Ingredients: Chicken breast/thigh, yogurt, Indian spices, tomatoes, cream, garlic, herbs.

Loved for its bold flavors and tenderness, perfect with rice or naan.

Beef

Our beef is slow-cooked to perfection using traditional techniques. Cuts are marinated in soy sauce, garlic, black pepper, and rosemary for hours, then braised or roasted depending on the dish.

Popular Dishes:
- Braised Beef Short Ribs
- Korean BBQ Bulgogi
- Classic Beef Stew

Main Ingredients: Beef chuck/ribs, soy sauce, onions, garlic, wine, herbs, carrots, potatoes.

Our beef menu is rich, hearty, and comforting — perfect for meat lovers.

Sushi

Our sushi is handcrafted fresh daily by trained chefs. We use premium sushi rice, fresh raw fish, seaweed, and traditional Japanese techniques to ensure authentic taste.

Popular Dishes:
- California Roll
- Tuna Nigiri
- Salmon Sashimi

Main Ingredients: Sushi rice, nori, salmon, tuna, avocado, cucumber, soy sauce, wasabi.

Perfect for light, healthy, and elegant dining experiences.
